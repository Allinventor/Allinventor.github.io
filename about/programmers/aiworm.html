What do I Create?

I create what I want to create. Yep, there's really nothing else to it. Apart from the fact that if someone has a great idea I also build it, but I give credit. This is what we ask for your name becaues it is the right thing to do. I can take credit for building it, and everything else I do. But if the idea isn't mine I wont take credit for that part. Plus it makes it even better because someone else wanted something and I was able to make it. I just like to have fun and creating brings out the good in me, regardless of what it is. However, I do have a few rules on what I create, and why.

What I create...
  1. can not be used to hurt others physically or emotionally
  2. must not deprive others of their basic freedoms
  3. must give as much control as physically possible to others (users)
  4. must have a failsafe (or more) built in for security
  5. must fit my personal morals
  6. must have the potential to inspire or motivate others
  7. must be approved by the code of conduct I created for myself with a score of 75% or higher
