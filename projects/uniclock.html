Project UniClock

A Universal Mutlipurpose Clock/Time Management Tool

"What is the best way to manage time? A clock? Alarm? Calendar? Reminders? Nope, all of it plus more." - Ai Worm

What is it?

It's a decentrailized clock and calendar combo designed to help you manage your time better. Why decentrailized? Because it's your personal events and reminders and they should stay as so.

What are the goals?

  • Allow for custom reminders
  • Allow for custom events
  • Allow for time-zone time comparison
  • Allow for Alarms with custom time and preferences
  • Allow Alarm to do certain things when they go off. (Luanch App, Play Music, Stop Music etc.)
  • Allow for more than one calanedar (Personal, School, Business etc.)
  • Allow for cross platform access
  • Provide Stopwatch
  • Provide Timer with custom events
