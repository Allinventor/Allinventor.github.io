Project Dani

Dictionaries Available Now Internationaly

"Is it possible to decentrailize the dictionary? Afterall, we are the ones who create and maintain words and the meaning they have towards us. But sometimes we just want to create a new word or a new offical meaning for an existing word. It would be nice to see if this could be possible. Who else is intrested?" - Ai Worm

What is it?

Dani is a decentrailized dictionary that is meant to be international. Meaning that every single word from around the world regardless of langauge is found in this archive. It is meant to help people agree or disagree on words and their definitions. It is meant as a tool to help us improve our vocabulary by being active in langauge. There is a lot I could say, but in the end the best thing about it is that, "we can rewrite langauge if we choose so".
